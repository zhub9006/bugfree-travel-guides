## 🛡️ Top Pro Tips

1. **Never skip permethrin-treated clothing**
2. **Always carry DEET/Picaridin 20%+**
3. **Stay on higher floors**
4. **Use portable fans**
5. **Know your seasonal windows**
6. **Book resorts, not local guesthouses**
7. **Choose coastal/windy spots**
8. **Inspect hotels on arrival**
