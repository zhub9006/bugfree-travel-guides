## 🎯 Top 12 Destinations (Detailed)

### 1. Antarctica — ⭐ Zero Bugs
**Best:** Nov–Mar | Temperature: -10°C to +5°C
- No parasitic insects; frozen desert; no mosquitoes, no flies, no ants

### 2. Iceland — ⭐ Nearly Zero
**Best:** Jun–Aug | Temperature: 5°C to 20°C
- Cold subarctic climate; no standing water; non-biting midges rare in late June

### 3. Svalbard — ⭐ Zero
**Best:** Jun–Sep | Temperature: -5°C to 10°C
- 78°N Arctic; permafrost; polar bears (not bugs) require armed guides

### 4. Faroe Islands — ⭐ Nearly Zero
**Best:** Jun–Aug | Temperature: 8°C to 15°C
- Remote 18 islands; Arctic wind; cold temps prevent colonization

### 5. Atacama Desert — ⭐ Zero
**Best:** Apr–Oct | Temperature: 5°C to 28°C
- Driest non-polar desert on Earth; zero moisture = zero insects

### 6. Gobi Desert — ⭐ Very Low
**Best:** Sep–Oct | Temperature: -5°C to 25°C
- Cold desert; no standing water; ticks May–Jul

### 7. Patagonia — ⭐ Very Low
**Best:** Nov–Mar | Temperature: 5°C to 20°C
- 60+ km/h winds; dry climate; flies near cattle Jan only

### 8. Maldives (Resorts) — ⭐⭐ Very Low
**Best:** Nov–Apr | Temperature: 28°C to 30°C
- Professional pest control; private islands; beach breezes

### 9. Curaçao — ⭐⭐ Very Low
**Best:** Jan–Sep | Temperature: 27°C to 29°C
- Trade winds; arid climate; west coast is driest

### 10. New Caledonia — ⭐⭐ Very Low
**Best:** May–Oct | Temperature: 20°C to 24°C
- French biosecurity; dry season insect-minimal

### 11. British Isles — ⭐ Low
**Best:** Sep–May | Temperature: 5°C to 20°C
- No dangerous insects; Edinburgh, Glasgow, London midge-free

### 12. Montserrat — ⭐ Very Low
**Best:** Jan–Apr | Temperature: 25°C to 30°C
- Volcanic terrain; natural drainage; no stagnant water

## 🛡️ Universal Tips

1. **Permethrin-treated clothing** — kills insects on contact
2. **DEET/Picaridin repellent (20%+)** — proven deterrent
3. **Stay on higher floors** — bugs travel upward
4. **Use fans** — mosquitoes can't fly in strong wind
5. **Avoid standing water at dusk** — peak mosquito time
6. **Choose cold/dry/windy destinations**
7. **Prefer islands & coastal spots**
8. **Book AC'd/screened accommodations**
